WHAT MAKES US THE BEST IN THE BUSINESS

Right Away Repairs was founded by Preston Pierce with a simple but powerful mission — to help others and bring honesty back into the construction industry.

After years of seeing homeowners and businesses overcharged or let down by unreliable service, Preston set out to build a company that puts people first. With a strong background in home repair and renovation, he started Right Away Repairs to provide quality workmanship at fair, transparent prices.

Every job is approached with integrity, skill, and care — because Preston believes that doing the right thing and helping others should always come first.
